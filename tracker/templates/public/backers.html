OpenGifters. How to become one?

You get unique bonuses for donating to projects on the Opengift Platform. Type of bonus depends on the amount of your donations.

Donator

> 1 000 GIFT

  • You can rate projects’ goals.

Backer

> 10 000 GIFT

  • You can write reviews for the projects and their goals.
  • You also obtain your own Expertise Rating.

OPENGIFTER

> 50 000 GIFT

  • You can set your own goals for the projects.
  • You can participate in projects’ boost-campaigns*.
  • You also get an OpenGift T-Shirt, a special badge and a souvenir GIFT-coin.

* A boost-campaign is implementation of a special smart-contract for the project in the blockchain according to which the TOP 5 project’s donators get a 1% share of the project by a certain date.
