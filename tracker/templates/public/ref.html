OpenGift referral program.

OpenGift is platform where everyone can set a direction for building open source software with donations. You can earn 20% of each donation you help to attract. This is what you need to do:
  • Sign up to opengift.io and register your wallet
  • Find a project or projects you'd like to support here
  • When you are on a project page press button "Get partners link"
  • Now you have a referral link and can place it everywhere you like; now each time anybody who come to a project page via your link make a donation, your receive your share on your GIFT wallet
